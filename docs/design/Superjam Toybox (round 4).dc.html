superjam · toybox — full screen set

Make a jam. Share the jam.

The complete flow in the Toybox identity, updated per Kristjan's notes: apps are called jams, discovery is a TikTok-style vertical feed, the making process shows a live preview instead of code or AI talk, and the tab bar is just Make · Discover · Inbox. Round 4 reorders the walkthrough to the real first-run — welcome → discover → make → inbox — and switches naming to the family model: you claim kris.superjam.eth at signup, and every jam you make mints under it (tipjarplus.kris.superjam.eth).

1 · Welcome

Email in, wallet appears — then claim your name

The front door, two beats. Type your email — Dynamic conjures a wallet behind it, no seed phrase, nothing to install. Then claim your name: kris.superjam.eth is minted right at signup, and from then on every jam you make hangs its own tag under it — tipjar.kris.superjam.eth, trivia.kris.superjam.eth… Your name is the family tree; the jams are its leaves.

Notes

Round 2 of Kristjan's comments applied: everything is a jam now, the plan screen has a refine loop (say a change, the plan updates), the making screen shows a live preview of the UI painting in (the stretch goal — worst case it degrades to the skeleton + steps), and the tab bar is down to Make · Discover · Inbox with the crew behind the profile chip. Sponsors locked as ENS + Dynamic + World, each with one hero moment in section 5: Dynamic owns onboarding (email → wallet appears), World ID makes reviews/comments human-only (badges on commenters and creators), and ENS covers both creator names (kris.eth, mira.superjam.eth) and jam subdomains (tipjarplus.superjam.eth) — plus the pills already woven through the reveal, feed, and crew screens. Next candidates: the judge deck in this identity, or a clickable prototype of the make → reveal → share loop.

Round 3 applies the sponsor deep-dive (DESIGN_SPONSORS.md): ✓-human stickers beside every @name, quiet mono name tags on turbojam.eth (punched-hole sticker on the reveal + feed), a remix lineage chip in the feed, an ENS step ("hanging the name tag") in the making list, a 🔒 secure-confirm mark on the money sheet — plus section 6's three new moments: the World ID trial gate, on-chain mode (cards flip to their chain-record backs), and the maker's badge. Where this deviates from the spec on purpose, see Spec feedback — sponsor surfaces.md.

Round 4: screens reordered to the real first-run — Welcome → Discover → Make → Crew & inbox — with money and the sponsor deep cuts after. Naming switched to the family model: your name (kris.superjam.eth) is minted at signup (new screen 1b), and every jam you make mints automatically under it — tipjarplus.kris.superjam.eth — so who-made-what is readable straight from the name. turbojam.eth references before this point are historical.
